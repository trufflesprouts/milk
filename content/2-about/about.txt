Approach:

-
  title: The Brief
  description: >
    During the initial phase of our
    collaboration, our time is dedicated to
    collecting as much information as
    possible to make your vision come to
    life. Absorbing everything necessary to
    create a brief that will then be sent to
    the creative team.
  icon: one.jpg
-
  title: The Research
  description: >
    This is when the process of analysis
    begins. Researching relevant topics,
    breaking down local and international
    competitors to make what it is that you
    are asking for as relevant and powerful
    as possible.
  icon: two.jpg
-
  title: The Execution
  description: >
    After analysis, phase 3 is taking all
    the gathered content to form a strong

    executions help us complete the projects
    at hand.
